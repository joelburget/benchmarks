About

The Organization

This application was developed by The Ohio State University Reusable Software Research Group.

Our research is primarily directed at modular verification of software.

The goal of this website is to propose problems and solutions to verified software benchmarks. A list of the original 9 benchmarks can be seen in our paper: Proceedings of VSTTE 2008 (Verified Software: Theories, Tools, and Experiments)

The Application

The Benchmarks application was built to help researchers across the globe connect and collaborate on problems, and their potential solutions, on the web.
